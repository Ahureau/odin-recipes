Vegan quinoa salad

This refreshing and hearty summertime salad features fresh herbs, vegetables, and a lemon dressing. Perfect for warm days!

Ingredients

  • Cooked quinoa
  • Chopped cucumber
  • Tomatoes
  • Parsley
  • Lemon dressing (olive oil, fresh lemon juice, mustard, garlic salt, and garlic)

Instructions

  1. Combine the cooked quinoa, chopped cucumber, tomatoes, and parsley in a large bowl.
  2. Prepare the lemon dressing by mixing olive oil, fresh lemon juice, mustard, garlic salt, and garlic in a mason jar.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well.
