Vegan lentil soup

This Vegan Blended Lentil Soup is a comforting bowl of goodness.

The red lentils create a velvety texture, while the aromatic spices like cumin and coriander add depth of flavor. The lemon juice provides a refreshing zing, making it a perfect choice for a light lunch or dinner. Enjoy it with crusty bread or a side salad for a complete meal! ðŸŒ±ðŸ¥£

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large carrot, peeled and diced
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, sautÃ© the chopped onion and minced garlic in a little olive oil until fragrant.
  2. Add the diced carrot and celery, and cook for a few minutes until slightly softened.
  3. Pour in the vegetable broth and add the rinsed red lentils.
  4. Season with ground cumin, ground coriander, salt, and pepper.
  5. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 20-25 minutes, until the lentils are tender.
  6. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until smooth and creamy. Alternatively, transfer the soup to a regular blender in batches and blend until smooth.
  7. Stir in the lemon juice for a bright, tangy flavor.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley or cilantro.
